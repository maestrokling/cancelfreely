Privacy Policy

CancelFreely collects no personal data. We use no analytics. We set no cookies. We don't track your browsing. We don't know who you are. We don't want to know who you are.

Our cancel database is open source and hosted on GitHub. The site is static HTML served through Cloudflare Pages. No server-side code processes your requests. No database stores your visits.

If you use the service request form, it opens a GitHub Issue in a public repository. GitHub's privacy policy applies to that interaction, not ours, because we have nothing to apply.

If you email us at info@cancelfreely.com, we receive your email. We don't add you to a list. We don't sell your address. We reply if a reply is needed and that's the end of it.

This site exists to help you take control of your data. We start by not taking any of it.
