How to Cancel Noom

Difficult (CFS 4/5)
Method: online or app · Last verified: 2025-10-01 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. In the Noom app: tap your profile > Settings > Subscription > Cancel Subscription.
  2. Online: go to noom.com > sign in > account settings > Subscription Portal > Cancel.
  3. Navigate through multiple retention screens — Noom will offer free trial extensions, deep discounts, and pause options before allowing cancellation. Stay firm. Multiple Reddit users report needing to decline 3-5 offers before reaching the final cancel button.
  4. Get a cancellation confirmation email — save it. Noom has been reported to continue charging after cancellation without confirmation.

Dark Patterns

  • stealth auto-renewal: Multiple Reddit users report Noom continued renewing charges after what they believed was a successful cancellation, with no renewal notification emails. One user described discovering months of charges they never received receipts for.
  • multi-screen retention maze: Noom's cancellation flow includes multiple retention screens with escalating offers (free trials, deep discounts, pauses) that must each be declined before reaching the final cancel button.
  • impossible-to-cancel reports: Reddit users describe Noom as 'impossible to cancel,' with phone agents transferring calls repeatedly and making them repeat their cancellation request multiple times.

Retention Tactics

  • Offers extended free trial (1-3 months).
  • Offers 50-80% discount on next renewal.
  • Offers a pause instead of canceling.

Free Alternatives

  • MyFitnessPal (free tier) — Free calorie tracking and food logging
  • Lose It! (free tier) — Free calorie and weight tracking
Notes: Noom is one of the most complained-about health app cancellations. Always get a confirmation email and check your credit card statement for the following month. If you're charged after cancellation, file a chargeback and contact Noom support with your cancellation confirmation.
