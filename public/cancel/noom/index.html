Cancellation Steps

  1. In the Noom app: tap your profile > Settings > Subscription > Cancel Subscription.
  2. Online: go to noom.com > sign in > account settings > Subscription Portal > Cancel.
  3. Navigate through multiple retention screens — Noom will offer free trial extensions, deep discounts, and pause options before allowing cancellation. Stay firm. Multiple Reddit users report needing to decline 3-5 offers before reaching the final cancel button.
  4. Get a cancellation confirmation email — save it. Noom has been reported to continue charging after cancellation without confirmation.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers extended free trial (1-3 months).
  • Offers 50-80% discount on next renewal.
  • Offers a pause instead of canceling.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
