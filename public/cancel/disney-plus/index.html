How to Cancel Disney+

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-01-21 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to disneyplus.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon, then select 'Account'.
  3. Under your subscription, click 'Cancel Subscription'.
  4. Confirm the cancellation when prompted. Access continues through the end of the billing period.

Dark Patterns

  • bundle upsell before cancel: Users may be shown the Disney Bundle (Disney+/Hulu/ESPN+) as a retention offer before cancellation is confirmed.
  • third-party billing maze: Users who subscribed via Apple App Store, Google Play, Amazon, or Roku cannot cancel on the Disney+ website — must cancel through the original billing platform.

Retention Tactics

  • Offers bundle downgrade instead of full cancellation.
  • Shows upcoming Marvel/Star Wars content to trigger FOMO.
