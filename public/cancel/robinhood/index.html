How to Cancel Robinhood

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: online or app · Last verified: 2026-01-23 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Before closing: sell or transfer all stocks, ETFs, and crypto. Withdraw all cash to your bank account. IRA accounts require a rollover to another institution. You cannot close a Robinhood account with a balance. All positions must be liquidated or transferred first.
  2. In the app: tap your profile icon > Account > Investing > Account Information > Deactivate Account.
  3. On web: go to account.robinhood.com > Settings > Investing > Deactivate Account.
  4. Send a written confirmation to support@robinhood.com stating your intent to close the account. The written email step is required per Robinhood's process. A representative will follow up within 5 business days.
