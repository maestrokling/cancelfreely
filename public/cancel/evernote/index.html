How to Cancel Evernote

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-02-17 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to evernote.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon, then go to 'Settings' > 'Billing'.
  3. Click 'Cancel subscription' and follow the prompts. If subscribed via Apple App Store or Google Play, cancel through those platforms instead.
  4. Your subscription remains active through the end of the billing period, then reverts to the free tier. The free tier limits you to 1 notebook and 50 notes. Export your notes before downgrading if you have more than that.
