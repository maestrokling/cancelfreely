How to Cancel QuickBooks

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-03-28 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Sign in at quickbooks.intuit.com.
  2. Click the gear icon (Settings) > Account and Settings > Billing & Subscription.
  3. Click 'Cancel subscription' and follow the prompts. For QuickBooks Self-Employed: same path, or go directly to quickbooks.intuit.com/self-employed.
  4. Export your data before canceling: Reports > export to Excel/CSV. Access ends at end of billing period. ⚠️ After cancellation, you lose access to your QuickBooks data. Export everything first.
