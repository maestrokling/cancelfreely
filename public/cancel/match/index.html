How to Cancel Match.com

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-02-17 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to match.com and sign in. If subscribed via Apple or Google, cancel through those platforms instead.
  2. Click your profile photo > Settings > Subscription.
  3. Find 'Cancel Subscription' or 'Manage Subscription' and follow the prompts. Match requires navigating through several menus — the cancel option is not prominently displayed.
  4. ⚠️ Canceling stops auto-renewal but your subscription remains active until the end of the paid term. Match does not issue refunds for unused time.

Dark Patterns

  • no-refund policy: Match.com does not refund unused subscription time after cancellation. Canceling stops future charges but does not recover the current period.
  • confusing 'cancel' framing: Match's cancel flow turns off auto-renewal but doesn't make clear that the subscription itself continues until the paid period ends. Users sometimes believe they've gotten a refund when they haven't.

Retention Tactics

  • May offer a discounted renewal.
