How to Cancel Paramount+

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-03-04 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to paramountplus.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon, then select 'Account'.
  3. Under 'Subscription', choose 'Paramount+', then select 'Cancel Subscription'.
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm. Access continues through the end of the billing period. If subscribed through Amazon, Apple, Roku, or another platform, cancel there instead.

Dark Patterns

  • third-party billing redirect: Subscribers through Amazon Channels, Apple, Roku, or cable providers must cancel through the originating platform. Paramount+ website won't show a cancel option for these subscribers.

Retention Tactics

  • May offer a pause or discounted rate before confirming cancellation.

Free Alternatives

  • Pluto TV — Owned by Paramount; free ad-supported tier with some Paramount content
  • Tubi — Free ad-supported streaming
