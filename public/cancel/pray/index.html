Cancellation Steps

  1. Determine where you originally subscribed — web, iOS App Store, or Google Play. You must cancel through the same platform you subscribed on.
  2. iOS: Go to Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions > Pray.com > Cancel Subscription.
  3. Android: Open Google Play > Profile > Payments & Subscriptions > Subscriptions > Pray.com > Cancel.
  4. Web: Go to pray.com, sign in, navigate to Account > Manage Subscription > Cancel. The web cancel option may be difficult to find — look under account settings or billing.
  5. Confirm cancellation. Access continues through the end of your billing period. Keep confirmation email or screenshot for your records.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ free trial auto-conversion

Pray.com offers a free trial that automatically converts to a paid annual subscription if not canceled before the trial ends. The trial-to-paid conversion is a common complaint.

⚠ difficult cancellation

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Free trial auto-converts to annual subscription, creating inertia.
  • May present inspirational content or daily prayer streaks to encourage continued use during the cancellation flow.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
