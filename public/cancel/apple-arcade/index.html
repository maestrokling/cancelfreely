How to Cancel Apple Arcade

Easy (CFS 1/5)
Method: device · Last verified: 2026-01-07 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. On iPhone/iPad: Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions > Apple Arcade > Cancel Subscription.
  2. On Mac: App Store > your profile icon > Subscriptions > Apple Arcade > Cancel.
  3. On Apple TV: Settings > Users and Accounts > [your name] > Subscriptions > Apple Arcade > Cancel.
