How to Cancel Adobe Creative Cloud

Very Hard (CFS 5/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-03-15 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to account.adobe.com and sign in.
  2. Click 'Plans' then select 'Manage plan' next to your Creative Cloud subscription.
  3. Click 'Cancel plan' and follow the prompts. CRITICAL: If you are within 14 days of purchase, cancellation is free. After 14 days, Adobe charges an Early Termination Fee of 50% of the remaining contract balance. On a $59.99/month annual plan, this can be hundreds of dollars.
  4. Navigate through multiple retention screens before reaching the final confirmation. Adobe will offer discounts, pauses, and downgrades before allowing cancellation.

Dark Patterns

  • hidden early termination fee: Adobe's annual plan (billed monthly) includes a 50% ETF on remaining balance if canceled after 14 days. This fee was hidden behind 'fine print and inconspicuous hyperlinks' at signup — the subject of a $150 million class action settlement in 2026.
  • bait-and-switch pricing: Adobe advertises introductory monthly rates (e.g., $34.99/month) that revert to full price ($69.99/month) after 3 months, with the full terms buried behind a 'view terms' link most users miss.
  • multi-screen retention maze: Adobe uses multiple screens with discount offers, plan pauses, and emotional appeals before allowing cancellation to proceed.

Retention Tactics

  • Offers a 2-3 month pause instead of canceling.
  • Offers a significant discount (up to 40%) to stay.
  • Offers a downgrade to a single-app plan.
  • Shows all the apps you'll lose access to.
