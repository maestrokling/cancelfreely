How to Cancel Grammarly

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2025-09-15 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to grammarly.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon, then go to 'Settings' > 'Subscription'.
  3. Click 'Cancel subscription' at the bottom of the page.
  4. Click 'Continue' in the confirmation window, then select a cancellation reason and click 'Cancel Subscription' to finalize. Access continues through the end of the billing period. You revert to the free tier after cancellation.
