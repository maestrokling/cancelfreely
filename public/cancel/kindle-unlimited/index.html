How to Cancel Kindle Unlimited

Simple — CFS 2/5
Method: online · Last verified: 2025-12-29 · Official site
Go to cancel page →

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to amazon.com/ku/manage and sign in. Direct URL: amazon.com/kindle-dbs/hz/subscribe/ku?ref=ku_mng_stng
  2. Click 'Cancel Kindle Unlimited Membership'.
  3. A confirmation screen will show your membership end date — click 'Cancel membership' to confirm. You can continue reading borrowed books until the end of the billing period. After that, borrowed titles are removed from your device.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ double confirmation

Amazon requires two separate confirmation clicks before completing cancellation.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.

Free Alternatives

  • Libby — Free ebooks and audiobooks through your public library
  • Project Gutenberg — Free public domain ebooks
  • Amazon Prime Reading — Limited free ebook selection included with Amazon Prime
Notes: Kindle Unlimited is one of the simpler Amazon cancellations — unlike Prime, it has its own dedicated management page. The double-confirmation is mildly annoying but not adversarial. Borrowed books are removed after the billing period ends; purchased Kindle books are unaffected.
