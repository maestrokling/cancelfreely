How to Cancel AAA

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: phone or in-person · Last verified: 2026-01-15 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Call your local AAA club (number varies by region — find it at aaa.com). AAA is a federation of regional clubs. Cancellation is handled by your local club, not a national center.
  2. Or visit a local AAA branch in person.
  3. Request cancellation and ask about your refund eligibility. Prorated refund is available IF: you have not used Emergency Roadside Services during the current term AND you meet any additional local club requirements.
