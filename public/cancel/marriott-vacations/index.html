How to Cancel Marriott Vacations Worldwide

Very Hard — CFS 5/5
Method: certified mail or official exit program · Last verified: 2026-03-05 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. CRITICAL: Check your contract for the rescission period — typically 3 to 10 days depending on the state where you purchased. This is your only penalty-free exit window.
  2. Within the rescission period: Write a rescission letter with your name, contract number, purchase date, and a clear statement that you are rescinding the contract. All signers must sign.
  3. Send via USPS Certified Mail with Return Receipt to the address in your contract. Postmark date controls the deadline. Keep the certified mail receipt and return receipt card. These are your proof of timely cancellation.
  4. After the rescission period: Contact Marriott Vacations' official exit program. Call Marriott Vacation Club owner services: 1-800-860-9384. Marriott offers a voluntary surrender/deed-back program for qualifying owners. Eligibility requirements apply.
  5. ⚠️ Avoid any third-party company charging upfront fees to exit your Marriott timeshare. Many are scams targeting timeshare owners.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Owner services may offer hardship programs, fee waivers, or point conversions as alternatives to exit.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.

Notes: Marriott Vacation Club (formerly MVCI) is a real estate contract, not a subscription. The Marriott Exit Program (voluntary surrender) requires the account to be in good standing with no outstanding loans. After the rescission window, your options are: official surrender program, resale (limited market, often near zero value), or licensed timeshare attorney. Marriott Vacations Worldwide also owns Sheraton Vacation Club, Westin Vacation Club, and Vistana Signature Experiences — exit processes are similar.
