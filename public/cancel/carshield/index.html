Cancellation Steps

  1. Call CarShield: 1-800-977-3450. Have your vehicle service contract number ready. CarShield does not allow online cancellation. Phone only.
  2. State you want to cancel your vehicle protection plan. Get a cancellation confirmation number.
  3. Refund terms: within 30 days = full refund. After 30 days = prorated refund minus a $75 cancellation fee. Processing time for refund: 45–60 days.
  4. Send a written cancellation request via certified mail to your contract administrator as a backup. Some CarShield plans are administered by third parties — your contract should specify who to contact.

Dark Patterns

  • phone-only cancellation: No online cancellation despite online signup. Phone required.
  • cancellation fee: CarShield charges a $75 cancellation fee after the 30-day free look period. This fee is buried in the contract terms.
  • FTC complaints: CarShield has faced significant FTC scrutiny and consumer complaints for misleading advertising (implying manufacturer relationship) and claims denials.
  • long refund processing: Prorated refunds take 45–60 days to process — significantly longer than most subscription services.

Retention Tactics

  • May offer a reduced monthly rate to retain you.
