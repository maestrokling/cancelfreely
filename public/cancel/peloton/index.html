How to Cancel Peloton

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2025-10-30 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to onepeloton.com and sign in. Cancellation cannot be done from the Peloton equipment itself — use the website.
  2. Click your profile icon, then select 'Membership'.
  3. Select 'Cancel Membership' and follow the prompts. You can cancel All-Access (equipment-linked) or the App subscription separately.
  4. Confirm cancellation. If you own Peloton equipment, you can still use it after canceling the subscription but will lose access to live classes and most on-demand content. Some limited free content remains accessible.
