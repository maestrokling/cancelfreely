Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to members.onepeloton.com and sign in. Cancellation cannot be done from the Peloton bike/tread touchscreen or the mobile app — you must use the website.
  2. Click your profile icon in the top right, then select 'Memberships'.
  3. Click 'Cancel Membership' next to the membership you want to cancel. You can cancel All-Access (equipment-linked, ~$44/month) or the App membership (~$12.99/month) separately.
  4. Walk through the cancellation flow — Peloton will present retention offers and alternatives. Peloton will offer a pause, a discounted rate, and show upcoming classes before giving you the final cancel button.
  5. Confirm cancellation. If you own Peloton equipment, it still functions for manual workouts after canceling. You lose access to live and most on-demand classes, but some limited free content remains.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers a membership pause (1-3 months) before allowing cancellation.
  • Presents a discounted rate (commonly 50% off for 2-3 months) to retain you.
  • Highlights upcoming live classes and instructor milestones to trigger FOMO.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.

Free Alternatives

  • YouTube fitness channels — Free cycling, running, strength, and yoga workouts
  • Nike Training Club — Free guided workouts across multiple categories
  • Apple Fitness+ (with Apple device) — Included free with some Apple devices, $9.99/month otherwise
