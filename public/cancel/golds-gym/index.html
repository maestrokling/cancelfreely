Cancellation Steps

  1. Determine whether your location is a corporate or franchise-owned club — this affects the cancellation process. Check goldsgym.com/cancellation-request/ — each location manages cancellations individually via a specific link.
  2. For corporate clubs: use the cancellation request page at goldsgym.com/cancellation-request/ and follow the link provided for your location.
  3. For franchise clubs: visit in person or send a written cancellation notice via certified mail to your club's address. Include your full name, membership ID, address, phone number, and cancellation request. Use certified mail with return receipt.
  4. Provide required notice — typically 30 days. Early termination fees may apply if under contract. Fees up to $75 reported. Month-to-month members generally cancel without fees.
