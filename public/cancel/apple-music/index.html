How to Cancel Apple Music

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to music.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID. On iPhone/iPad: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Apple Music > Cancel Subscription.
  2. Click the My Account icon, then select 'Settings'.
  3. Scroll to 'Subscriptions' and click 'Manage'.
  4. Select Apple Music and click 'Cancel Subscription'. Confirm when prompted. Music access ends at the end of the billing period. Downloaded songs become unplayable after cancellation.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
