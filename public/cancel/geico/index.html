How to Cancel GEICO

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: phone · Last verified: 2026-03-01 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. IMPORTANT: Get your new insurance policy in place BEFORE canceling GEICO. Never cancel without active replacement coverage — driving uninsured is illegal and creates a coverage gap that raises future premiums.
  2. Call GEICO: 1-800-841-1587. GEICO does not allow online or app cancellation. Phone is the only method.
  3. Tell the representative you want to cancel your policy. Provide your policy number, the cancellation date (typically your new policy's start date), and the reason.
  4. GEICO will prorate your refund for any unused premium — typically mailed as a check or credited to your payment method within 10-14 business days. Keep your cancellation confirmation number.
