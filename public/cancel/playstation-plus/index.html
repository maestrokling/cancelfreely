How to Cancel PlayStation Plus

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: online or console · Last verified: 2026-02-13 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. On PS5/PS4: Settings > Account Management > Account Information > PlayStation Subscriptions > select PS Plus > Turn Off Auto-Renewal.
  2. Online: Go to playstation.com > Account > Subscriptions > PS Plus > Cancel.
  3. Subscription continues through end of paid period. Monthly PS Plus games you claimed remain in your library as long as you maintain any PS Plus tier. ⚠️ If you re-subscribe later, claimed games return — but games are lost if you never re-subscribe.

Dark Patterns

  • cancellation controversy (2025): PlayStation Plus faced significant cancellation complaints in 2025, with Sony investigated in multiple jurisdictions for dark patterns in its cancellation flow, including hiding the cancel option behind obscure menu paths.
  • game access lock-in: Monthly PS Plus games claimed while subscribed become inaccessible if the subscription lapses — creating strong lock-in pressure to maintain the subscription.

Retention Tactics
