Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to namecheap.com and sign in to your account.
  2. For domains: Go to Domain List > find the domain > click 'Manage' > toggle 'Auto-Renew' off. The domain remains active until its expiration date and simply won't be renewed.
  3. For hosting products: Go to Hosting List > find the hosting plan > click 'Manage' > Cancel or turn off auto-renewal. Back up your website files and databases before canceling hosting.
  4. For other products (SSL, VPN, email): navigate to the specific product dashboard and disable auto-renewal or cancel. Domains that are not renewed enter a grace period (typically 30 days), then a redemption period (30 days at an additional fee), before full release.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Sends multiple email reminders before domain expiration with renewal discount offers.
  • Grace and redemption periods act as a safety net but also create urgency to renew at full price.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
