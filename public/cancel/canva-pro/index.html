How to Cancel Canva Pro

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2025-10-09 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to canva.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon, then select 'Account settings'.
  3. Go to 'Billing & Teams' then click 'Change plan'. For Canva Teams/Business accounts, only the owner or admin can cancel.
  4. Select the free plan or 'Cancel plan' option. Canva may offer a pause option at this screen.
  5. Confirm cancellation. Access continues through the end of the billing period. Pro assets (photos, elements, fonts) used in designs become restricted after downgrade — designs won't be deleted but Pro-only elements will show as locked.

Dark Patterns

  • Pro asset lock-in: After downgrading from Pro, any designs using Pro-licensed assets (photos, elements, fonts) have those elements locked. Designs remain but become visually incomplete, creating pressure to resubscribe.

Retention Tactics

  • Offers a 1-month pause instead of canceling.
  • Shows Pro features you'll lose access to.

Free Alternatives

  • Canva (free tier) — Generous free tier with thousands of templates
  • Adobe Express (free tier) — Free design tool with templates
Notes: Canva Pro cancellation is straightforward. The main thing to know: Pro-licensed assets in existing designs become locked after downgrade. If you're downgrading, export finished designs as PDFs/PNGs before your billing period ends.
