How to Cancel OneDrive (Microsoft 365)

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-01-23 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to account.microsoft.com and sign in. OneDrive storage plans are bundled with Microsoft 365. Canceling Microsoft 365 also cancels the extra storage.
  2. Go to 'Services & subscriptions' > find your Microsoft 365 plan > 'Manage' > 'Cancel subscription'.
  3. After cancellation, OneDrive reverts to the free 5GB tier. Files above that limit become read-only and then inaccessible after a grace period. ⚠️ Microsoft is ending standalone SharePoint Online and OneDrive plans as of May 2026 — only Microsoft 365 bundles will be available going forward.

Free Alternatives

  • Google Drive (free 15GB) — Free 15GB
Notes: Standalone OneDrive storage plans are being discontinued by Microsoft (new sales end May 31, 2026). Storage above 5GB requires a Microsoft 365 subscription. After cancellation, download any files above the 5GB limit before they become inaccessible.
