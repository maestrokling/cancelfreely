Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to godaddy.com and sign in.
  2. Go to your account > Renewals & Billing > find the product > turn off Auto-Renew. GoDaddy has many separate product lines — domains, hosting, email, website builder. Each cancels separately.
  3. For refunds within 30 days: contact GoDaddy support at 1-480-505-8877 or via chat.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ upsell-heavy checkout

GoDaddy is notorious for aggressive upselling during domain purchase — privacy protection, SSL, hosting, email, website builder, all pre-checked or prominently pushed. Many users end up with services they didn't intend to buy.

⚠ multiple separate billing items

GoDaddy bills each service separately with different renewal dates, making it easy to lose track of what you're paying for. Turning off auto-renew on a domain doesn't affect hosting, and vice versa.
