How to Cancel Dollar Shave Club

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2025-12-19 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to dollarshaveclub.com and sign in.
  2. Go to 'Account' > 'Next Box' tab.
  3. Find the subscription you want to cancel and select 'Cancel'. Individual products can be canceled separately. You can also cancel the whole membership.
  4. Confirm cancellation. If you can't find the cancel option, use live chat at dollarshaveclub.com or call support.
