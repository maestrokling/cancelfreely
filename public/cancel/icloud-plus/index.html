How to Cancel iCloud+

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: device or online · Last verified: 2025-11-18 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. On iPhone/iPad: Go to Settings > [your name] > iCloud > Manage Account Storage > Change Storage Plan > Downgrade Options > select Free.
  2. On Mac: System Settings > [your name] > iCloud > Manage > Change Storage Plan > Downgrade Options.
  3. Without an Apple device: Go to appleid.apple.com, sign in, then manage subscriptions.
  4. ⚠️ Downgrading takes effect at the end of the billing period. Files above the free 5GB limit won't be deleted immediately, but new uploads and iCloud backups will stop working until you're under the limit.
