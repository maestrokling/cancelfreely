How to Cancel OnStar

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: online or phone · Last verified: 2026-03-02 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Online: go to onstar.com, sign in to your account, and navigate to 'Manage Plan' > 'Cancel Service'. OnStar says online cancellation is available, but some users report the dashboard doesn't surface a clear cancel option.
  2. By phone: press the blue OnStar button in your vehicle and request cancellation, or call 1-888-466-7827.
  3. State clearly you want to cancel — agents will make retention offers. OnStar charges $25-$50+/month depending on plan tier.

Dark Patterns

  • retention-first phone flow: OnStar phone cancellation routes through retention specialists who make multiple offers. Users report needing to decline discounts and downgrades before cancellation is processed.
  • dashboard cancel inconsistency: OnStar claims online cancellation is available but users and third-party guides report the dashboard does not clearly surface a cancel option, effectively requiring a phone call.

Retention Tactics

  • Offers a downgrade to a lower-tier plan.
  • Offers a discounted rate for 6-12 months.

Free Alternatives

  • Your smartphone — Most OnStar features (navigation, emergency call) are replicated by smartphone + carrier emergency services
Notes: OnStar is built into GM vehicles (Chevy, GMC, Buick, Cadillac). Many features work without a paid plan — basic emergency calling may be free. The $25-50/month plans add remote start, diagnostics, navigation, and 4G WiFi hotspot. After cancellation, the hardware remains in the vehicle but services are deactivated.
