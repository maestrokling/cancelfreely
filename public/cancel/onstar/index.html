Cancellation Steps

  1. Online: go to onstar.com, sign in to your account, and navigate to 'Manage Plan' > 'Cancel Service'. OnStar says online cancellation is available, but some users report the dashboard doesn't surface a clear cancel option.
  2. By phone: press the blue OnStar button in your vehicle and request cancellation, or call 1-888-466-7827.
  3. State clearly you want to cancel — agents will make retention offers. OnStar charges $25-$50+/month depending on plan tier.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers a downgrade to a lower-tier plan.
  • Offers a discounted rate for 6-12 months.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
