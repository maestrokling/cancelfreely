Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to crunchyroll.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon > 'Manage Premium' > 'Cancel Membership'. If subscribed via Apple, Google, Amazon, or Roku, you must cancel through that platform instead.
  3. Select a cancellation reason and confirm. Access continues through end of billing period.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ free tier elimination

Crunchyroll eliminated its free ad-supported tier on December 31, 2025, removing the fallback option that previously let users watch with ads. Cancellation now means losing all access to the catalog.

⚠ platform-specific cancellation

Users who subscribed through third-party platforms (Apple, Google, Roku, Amazon) cannot cancel through the Crunchyroll website — they must navigate to the platform's subscription management, which many users don't realize.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Shows upcoming seasonal anime releases you'll miss.
  • May offer a downgrade to a lower tier (Fan vs Mega Fan).

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
