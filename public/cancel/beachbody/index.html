How to Cancel Beachbody / BODi

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: online or phone · Last verified: 2026-05-28 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to bodi.com (formerly beachbodyondemand.com) and sign in. The service rebranded from Beachbody On Demand to BODi.
  2. Go to your account settings and navigate to 'Subscription' or 'Manage Membership'.
  3. Select 'Cancel Subscription' and follow the prompts. Canceling stops future renewals but does NOT remove current access — you retain access through the end of your paid period.
  4. If you cannot find the cancel option online, call customer service: 1-800-470-7870. Phone cancellation is available but be prepared for retention offers.
