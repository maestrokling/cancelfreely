Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to bodi.com (formerly beachbodyondemand.com) and sign in. The service rebranded from Beachbody On Demand to BODi.
  2. Go to your account settings and navigate to 'Subscription' or 'Manage Membership'.
  3. Select 'Cancel Subscription' and follow the prompts. Canceling stops future renewals but does NOT remove current access — you retain access through the end of your paid period.
  4. If you cannot find the cancel option online, call customer service: 1-800-470-7870. Phone cancellation is available but be prepared for retention offers.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ opaque renewal dates

Multiple users report not receiving adequate advance notice of renewal charges, especially on annual plans. Users advise checking renewal dates manually in account settings.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • May offer a discounted renewal rate.
  • Highlights workout programs you haven't tried.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
