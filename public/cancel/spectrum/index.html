How to Cancel Spectrum (Charter)

Difficult — CFS 4/5
Method: phone or in-store · Last verified: 2026-02-15 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Call Spectrum: 1-833-267-6094 (retention/disconnect line). Spectrum does not allow online cancellation. Phone or in-person only.
  2. Alternatively, visit a Spectrum store in person — faster than calling.
  3. State clearly you want to cancel. Retention agents will make multiple offers — discounts, speed upgrades, bundle renegotiations. Spectrum has no early termination fee on standard residential plans — a genuine advantage over some competitors.
  4. Return all rented equipment (modem, router, cable box) to a Spectrum store or UPS drop-off. Keep the receipt. IMPORTANT: Spectrum does NOT prorate bills. Canceling mid-billing-cycle means you pay the full month — time your cancellation to the end of your billing period if possible.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers significant discounts (often 50%+ off) on current plan.
  • Offers speed upgrades at same price.
  • Offers service bundling to lower per-service cost.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
