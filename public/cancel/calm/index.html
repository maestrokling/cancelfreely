Cancellation Steps

  1. ⚠️ Uninstalling the Calm app does NOT cancel your subscription.
  2. iOS: Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions > Calm > Cancel Subscription.
  3. Android: Google Play > Profile > Payments & Subscriptions > Subscriptions > Calm > Cancel.
  4. Web (if subscribed directly): calm.com > Profile > Settings > Manage Subscription > Cancel.
