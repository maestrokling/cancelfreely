How to Cancel Progressive

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: phone or online · Last verified: 2026-02-10 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. IMPORTANT: Have your new policy active before canceling. Never have a gap in coverage.
  2. Online: Log in at progressive.com, go to 'My Policy', then look for 'Cancel Policy'. Online cancellation availability varies by state and policy type. If not available online, call.
  3. By phone: Call 1-888-671-4405.
  4. Specify your cancellation effective date (usually your new policy's start date). Progressive will refund unused premium. Short-rate cancellation fees may apply in some states if you cancel mid-term — check your policy.
