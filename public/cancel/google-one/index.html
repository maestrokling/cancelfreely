How to Cancel Google One

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online or app · Last verified: 2026-03-01 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to one.google.com and sign in.
  2. Click 'Manage' next to your storage plan, then select 'Cancel plan'.
  3. On Android: Open Google One app > Settings > Manage Google One > Cancel membership.
  4. Access reverts to the free 15GB tier. Files above that limit become inaccessible to upload/edit — but existing files are not deleted for a grace period. Prorated refund is available if you request it immediately after canceling.
