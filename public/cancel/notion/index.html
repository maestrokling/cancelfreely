How to Cancel Notion

Easy (CFS 1/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-03-15 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Open Notion on desktop (web or app) and sign in. Cannot cancel from the mobile app.
  2. Click 'Settings' in the left sidebar.
  3. Go to 'Billing' then click 'Change plan'.
  4. Select the free plan to downgrade/cancel. Notion reverts to the free tier — all your content is preserved. No data is deleted.
