How to Cancel Backblaze

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-03-02 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to backblaze.com and sign in.
  2. Go to 'My Account' > 'Overview' > find your backup license and click 'Remove'. For Computer Backup: remove all registered backups and licenses. For B2 Cloud Storage: the account can be canceled separately.
  3. To fully delete your account: go to 'My Account' > 'Account Details' > 'Close Account'.
