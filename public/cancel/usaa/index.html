Cancellation Steps

  1. IMPORTANT: Have replacement insurance active before canceling. Never have a coverage gap. USAA is restricted to military members, veterans, and their families. If you're canceling insurance (not membership), secure a new policy first.
  2. Call USAA: 1-800-531-8722. USAA requires phone contact to cancel insurance policies. Online and app cancellation is not available.
  3. Specify the policy and cancellation date. Unused premium is refunded pro-rata.
