Cancellation Steps

  1. Call Fabletics: 1-844-322-5384 (24/7). This is the primary cancellation method. Fabletics requires phone or live chat to cancel the VIP membership — no self-service online cancel.
  2. Alternatively use live chat at fabletics.com.
  3. State clearly you want to cancel the VIP membership. Agents will make retention offers — decline them.
  4. ⚠️ Skip by the 5th of each month or you are charged $59.95 as a 'member credit.' If you have accumulated credits, use them before canceling.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ phone-only cancellation

Despite a fully online signup, Fabletics requires phone or chat to cancel the VIP membership. No self-service cancel option exists online.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers VIP credits to stay.
  • Offers skip options going forward.
  • May offer a reduced rate.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
