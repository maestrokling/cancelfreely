Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to theathletic.com and sign in. The Athletic is now owned by the New York Times. If you have a combined NYT + Athletic subscription, manage it at nytimes.com/athletic/settings.
  2. Click your profile icon, then go to 'Settings' > 'Manage Subscription'.
  3. Select 'Cancel Subscription' and follow the prompts. Access continues through end of billing period.
