Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to dropbox.com and sign in. If you purchased via mobile app store, you must cancel through Apple App Store or Google Play instead.
  2. Click your avatar in the top right, then select 'Settings'.
  3. Click the 'Plan' tab, then select 'Cancel plan'.
  4. Dropbox shows a final retention screen — click 'Cancel subscription' to confirm. Storage reverts to the free 2GB tier after cancellation. Files above that limit remain in your account but become inaccessible until storage is within the free limit.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ mobile billing redirect

Users who subscribed via iOS or Android cannot cancel on the Dropbox website — must cancel through the originating app store.
