How to Cancel TruGreen

Moderate — CFS 3/5
Method: phone · Last verified: 2026-03-09 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Call TruGreen: 1-800-464-0171.
  2. State clearly you want to cancel your lawn care plan. Have your account number ready. Agents will make retention offers. Be direct: 'I want to cancel, please do not schedule any more services.'
  3. Confirm your cancellation date and that no further services will be performed. Get a confirmation number.
  4. Watch your property for the next 2-4 weeks. TruGreen has documented cases of continuing service after cancellation — verify no technicians visit.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ service after cancellation

TruGreen has a well-documented pattern of continuing to send technicians and bill customers after cancellation. Reddit and BBB have multiple accounts of customers canceling and then finding TruGreen treated their lawn anyway — and sent a bill.

TruGreen auto-enrolls customers into the next year's plan at the end of the season. Many customers don't realize they've been re-enrolled until they receive a spring service they didn't request.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers a re-application visit for free.
  • Offers a discounted rate for the remainder of the season.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
