Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to betterhelp.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon > 'Account Settings'.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click 'Cancel membership'.
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm. You'll receive a confirmation email. Consider your ongoing therapy needs before canceling. If you want to pause rather than cancel, BetterHelp allows pauses of up to 4 weeks.

⚠ FTC settlement (2023)

BetterHelp paid $7.8M in FTC refunds in 2023 for sharing user mental health data with Facebook and Snapchat for advertising despite promising not to. This was a data privacy issue, not a cancellation dark pattern, but is material context.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers a pause instead of canceling.
  • May offer a financial hardship rate.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
