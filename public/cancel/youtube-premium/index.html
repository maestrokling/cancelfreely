How to Cancel YouTube Premium

Simple — CFS 2/5
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-04-11 · Official site
Go to cancel page →

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to youtube.com/paid_memberships and sign in. On mobile: tap your profile picture > Paid memberships.
  2. Select YouTube Premium.
  3. Click 'Manage' then 'Cancel membership'. If you subscribed via Google Play, Apple, or another platform, you must cancel through that platform instead.
  4. Confirm cancellation. Access continues through the end of the billing period. Ads return immediately after cancellation date.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • May offer a pause option before confirming cancellation.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.

Free Alternatives

  • YouTube (free) — Full YouTube access with ads — same content, no offline or background play
  • uBlock Origin (browser extension) — Free ad blocker for desktop browsers
Notes: YouTube Premium is one of the more straightforward cancels. The free tier of YouTube retains all content access — you lose ad-free viewing, background play, and offline downloads.
