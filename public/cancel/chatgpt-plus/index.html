How to Cancel ChatGPT Plus

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-01-13 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to chat.openai.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon in the bottom left, then select 'Settings'.
  3. Go to 'Account' (not the 'Manage' button under payments — use the first 'Manage' button in the subscription section). Users report confusion between two 'Manage' buttons — use the one directly under your subscription details, not the payments section.
  4. Click 'Cancel plan' and confirm. Access continues through the end of the billing period. Reverts to the free tier (GPT-4o with usage limits).
