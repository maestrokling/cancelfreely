Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online or app · Last verified: 2026-03-06 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Open the Acorns app or go to app.acorns.com and sign in.
  2. Tap your profile icon > Settings > Personal > Subscription > Close Account.
  3. Follow the prompts to withdraw your balance and confirm account closure. Your invested balance will be liquidated and returned to your linked bank account. This may take 3-5 business days.
