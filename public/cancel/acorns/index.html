Cancellation Steps

  1. Open the Acorns app or go to app.acorns.com and sign in. Account closure must be done through the app or website — you cannot call to cancel.
  2. Tap your profile icon > Settings > Personal > Subscription > Close Account.
  3. Follow the prompts to withdraw your balance and confirm account closure. Your invested balance will be liquidated (sold) and returned to your linked bank account. This may take 3-6 business days.
  4. Confirm the tax implications of liquidating your investments. Selling investments may trigger capital gains taxes if your positions have appreciated. Consider timing your closure accordingly.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ forced liquidation

To close your Acorns account, all investments must be sold — you cannot transfer holdings to another brokerage. This creates a taxable event and a psychological barrier to leaving.

⚠ multi-step guilt flow

The cancellation flow shows your investment progress, projected growth, and how much you'll 'lose' by leaving, creating emotional friction before allowing closure.

⚠ no investment transfer

Unlike traditional brokerages that support ACAT transfers, Acorns does not allow transferring your portfolio to another broker — you must sell everything and start over.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Shows projected future value of investments to create fear of missing out.
  • May offer a free month or reduced subscription rate during cancellation flow.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
