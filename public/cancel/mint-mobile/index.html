How to Cancel Mint Mobile

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online or phone · Last verified: 2026-03-01 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Port your number to the new carrier to automatically cancel Mint Mobile service. Number porting is the cleanest cancellation path — initiate at the new carrier. You'll need your Mint Mobile account number and PIN.
  2. To cancel without porting: log in at mintmobile.com > Account > Cancel Service.
  3. Or call Mint Mobile: 1-800-683-7392.
  4. 7-day refund window: if within 7 days of purchase, you can get a full refund.
