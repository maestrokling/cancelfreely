Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to sling.com and sign in to your account. Cannot be canceled from the Sling TV app on streaming devices.
  2. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner, then select 'Account'.
  3. Scroll down to 'Cancel Subscription' and click it. Sling will present a pause option first -- you must click past it to reach full cancellation.
  4. Sling will offer to pause your subscription (up to 3 months at $5/month). Click 'Continue to Cancel' to skip this. The pause option preserves your DVR recordings and settings.
  5. Select a cancellation reason and confirm. Access continues through the end of the billing period. DVR recordings are deleted after cancellation. Download any recordings you want to keep first (if applicable).

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ forced pause offer

Before allowing full cancellation, Sling forces users through a pause offer screen at $5/month, requiring an extra click to reach the actual cancel button.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers a discounted pause at $5/month for up to 3 months instead of full cancellation.
  • May offer a temporary discount or free month to retain subscribers who proceed past the pause screen.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
