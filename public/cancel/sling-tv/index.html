How to Cancel Sling TV

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-01-23 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to sling.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon > 'Account' > scroll to 'Cancel Subscription'.
  3. Sling will offer a pause option (up to 3 months, $5/month). If you want to fully cancel, click 'Continue to Cancel'.
  4. Confirm cancellation. Access continues through the end of the billing period.
