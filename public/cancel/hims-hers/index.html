Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to hims.com (or hers.com) and sign in.
  2. Go to your profile > 'Subscriptions' > select the subscription to cancel > 'Cancel Subscription'.
  3. ⚠️ Cancel at least 48 hours before your next order date to avoid being charged and shipped the next order. Hims processes and ships orders before the billing date. Missing the 48-hour window means you'll receive and be charged for the next shipment.
  4. Verify cancellation — some users report the account shows 'canceled' but a next refill date still appears. Confirm with support if unclear.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ confusing cancellation confirmation

Multiple BBB complaints describe accounts showing as 'canceled' while still displaying a next refill date — creating confusion about whether cancellation was processed. Some users were charged and shipped after believing they had canceled.
