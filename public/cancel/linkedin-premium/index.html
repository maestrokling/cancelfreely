Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to linkedin.com and sign in. If you subscribed via Apple App Store or iTunes, you must cancel through Apple — LinkedIn's website will not show a cancel option.
  2. Click your profile icon, then select 'Premium features' or go directly to linkedin.com/premium/manage.
  3. Click 'Manage subscription' then select 'Cancel subscription'. The cancel option is intentionally less prominent than the 'Keep my Premium' option.
  4. Select a reason and navigate through the retention screens to confirm cancellation. Access continues through the end of the billing period. InMail credits and premium insights are lost.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ free trial auto-enrollment

LinkedIn's free trials auto-enroll into paid subscriptions without prominent advance warning before the charge. Users report being charged without realizing their trial ended.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers a discounted renewal rate.
  • Shows InMail credits and job search insights you'll lose.
  • Highlights recent profile views from recruiters.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
