Cancellation Steps

  1. Fastest method: Port your number to the new carrier. Number porting automatically cancels T-Mobile service. If you're switching carriers, initiate the port at the new carrier — you do not need to call T-Mobile first.
  2. To cancel without porting: Call T-Mobile at 1-877-453-1304 or visit a T-Mobile store.
  3. Say clearly you want to cancel. T-Mobile's support page explicitly says 'we'd like to prevent your cancellation' — retention offers will follow.
  4. Check for remaining device installment balance — it must be paid even after service cancellation.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers discounted plan alternatives.
  • Offers account pause (up to 3 months).
  • Offers switching credits if reason is competitor pricing.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
