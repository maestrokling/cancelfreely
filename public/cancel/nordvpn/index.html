How to Cancel NordVPN

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-01-16 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to nordvpn.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon, then go to 'My Account' > 'Billing' > 'Manage Subscription'.
  3. Select 'Cancel Subscription' and confirm. Access continues through the end of the billing period.
  4. If within 30 days of purchase: request a full refund via live chat at nordvpn.com/contact-us. NordVPN's 30-day money-back guarantee applies to new subscriptions. Refund is processed to original payment method.
