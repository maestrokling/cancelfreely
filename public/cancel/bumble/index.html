How to Cancel Bumble

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: app store or web · Last verified: 2026-01-23 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. ⚠️ Deleting the Bumble app does NOT cancel your subscription.
  2. iOS: Open Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions > Bumble > Cancel Subscription.
  3. Android: Open Google Play > Profile > Payments & Subscriptions > Subscriptions > Bumble > Cancel.
  4. Web: Go to bumble.com > Profile photo > Manage your Boost or Manage your Premium > Cancel. Web cancellation is available for direct/web subscriptions.
