How to Cancel EA Play

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online or console · Last verified: 2026-02-21 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. On PC (EA App): go to ea.com/manage-membership/cancel and sign in.
  2. On PS4/PS5: Settings > Account Management > Account Information > PlayStation Subscriptions > select EA Play > Turn Off Auto-Renewal.
  3. On Xbox: account.microsoft.com > Services & subscriptions > EA Play > Cancel.
  4. On Steam: Library > Subscriptions > EA Play > Edit > Cancel my subscription.

Notes: EA Play cancellation method depends on where you subscribed. EA Play Pro (PC only, higher tier) cancels through the EA App. Games accessed through EA Play's vault become unplayable after cancellation.
