How to Cancel The Economist

Difficult (CFS 4/5)
Method: phone or email · Last verified: 2026-02-15 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to economist.com and sign in, then go to your account/subscription settings. Online self-service cancellation is not available for most subscribers.
  2. Use live chat (when available) at economist.com/help, or email customerservices@economist.com.
  3. Alternatively, call The Economist customer service (number varies by region — find it on their help page). US: 1-800-456-6086
  4. State you want to cancel. Agents will attempt retention offers including extended free trials.

Dark Patterns

  • no self-service cancellation: The Economist does not offer a straightforward online self-service cancel button for most subscribers. Users must contact support via chat, email, or phone. Trustpilot reviewers describe taking up to 30 minutes to cancel a free trial via chat.
  • multi-screen retention flow: During cancellation, multiple screens communicate the 'value' of the subscription. The continue-to-cancel button becomes progressively less prominent as you advance.

Retention Tactics

  • Offers an extended free trial (1-2 months) to delay cancellation.
  • Offers a heavily discounted renewal rate.
