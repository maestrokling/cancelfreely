Cancellation Steps

  1. Press the blue OnStar button in your GM vehicle and request cancellation, OR call 1-888-4ONSTAR (1-888-466-7827). See also the dedicated OnStar entry at cancelfreely.com/cancel/onstar for the full OnStar-specific guide.
  2. For other GM connected services (WiFi hotspot, Remote Access, Super Cruise): go to account.gm.com > Subscriptions > manage individual services.
  3. State which service(s) you want to cancel. Agents will make retention offers.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ multiple subscription layers

GM vehicles may have several separate connected service subscriptions: OnStar safety/security, WiFi hotspot (AT&T), Remote Access (app features), and Super Cruise (driver assist). Each may bill separately and cancel separately. Users often don't know how many they have.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers discounted rates for individual services.
  • Offers to keep one service while canceling others.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.

Notes: GM connected services cover OnStar, WiFi hotspot, Remote Access, and Super Cruise across Chevrolet, GMC, Buick, and Cadillac brands. Some services are free trials that auto-convert — check account.gm.com to see all active subscriptions. The OnStar entry in our database covers the primary safety/security subscription in more detail.
