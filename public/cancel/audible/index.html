Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to audible.com and sign in. Cancellation is NOT available through the Audible mobile app — desktop/web only.
  2. Click your profile name in the top right, then select 'Account details'.
  3. Under 'Membership details', click 'Cancel membership'. If you don't see this option, check which region's site you're on. UK users must use audible.co.uk, US users must use audible.com. Being on the wrong regional site is a known trap.
  4. Navigate through Audible's retention flow — you will be offered credits, pauses, and discounted plans before the final cancel button appears.
  5. Select a cancellation reason and click 'Cancel membership' to confirm. Any unused credits/coins may be lost. Keep in mind books already purchased remain in your library forever.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ mobile app exclusion

Cancellation is not available through the Audible mobile app — users must use a desktop browser, creating friction for mobile-first users.

⚠ regional redirect loop

US users accessing audible.co.uk (or vice versa) get stuck in a loop where the cancel option never appears. This is a known dark pattern reported by users as recently as January 2026.

⚠ UX complexity

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers to pause membership for 1-3 months.
  • Offers bonus credits to stay.
  • Offers a reduced membership rate.
  • Reminds you of your unused credits that will be lost.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
