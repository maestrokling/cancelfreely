Cancellation Steps

  1. For residential service: Call WM Customer Service at 1-800-592-9995 (Mon-Fri 7am-7pm, Sat 9am-1pm) or email via wm.com. For commercial service: the process differs significantly — see notes below.
  2. State you want to cancel and provide your account number and service address.
  3. Arrange return or pickup of WM-owned bins/containers if applicable. Keeping their equipment may result in additional charges.
  4. Get a cancellation confirmation and final bill amount. Check for any early termination fees in your service agreement.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ commercial contract auto-renewal with evergreen clause

WM commercial contracts contain 'evergreen' auto-renewal clauses — if you don't cancel within a specific window before the end of the contract term (often 60-90 days), the contract auto-renews for another full term (1-3 years). Missing this window locks you in. This is one of the most documented contract traps in commercial waste services.

⚠ price escalation

WM contracts allow for annual price increases (fuel surcharges, environmental fees, rate adjustments) that can significantly increase your effective rate over the contract term.

Free Alternatives

  • Local municipal waste service — Check if your municipality provides residential collection — often cheaper
Notes: Residential WM cancellation is relatively straightforward — call or email, arrange container return. Commercial WM contracts are a different matter entirely: read your contract for the auto-renewal notice window (often 60-90 days before contract end) and mark your calendar. Missing that window means another full term. If you're a commercial customer who missed the window, consult a contract attorney.
