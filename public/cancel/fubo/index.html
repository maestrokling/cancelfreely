How to Cancel Fubo

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-01-28 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to fubo.tv and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon > 'Account' > 'Manage Account' > 'Cancel Subscription'. If subscribed via Apple, Roku, Amazon, or another platform, cancel through that platform.
  3. Fubo may present a pause option or retention offer — click through to reach the final cancel confirmation.
