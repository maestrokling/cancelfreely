How to Cancel Stitch Fix

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online or chat · Last verified: 2026-01-21 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to stitchfix.com and sign in.
  2. Go to 'Account' > 'Styling Preferences' and change delivery frequency to 'I'll request my own Fixes' (on-demand). This stops automatic shipments without closing your account.
  3. To fully close/delete your account: Use the 'Chat' function in the app to reach customer service and request account deletion. Reddit users recommend being explicit: 'Cancel my account, do not just suspend deliveries.'
