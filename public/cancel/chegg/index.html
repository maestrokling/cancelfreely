Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to chegg.com and sign in.
  2. Go to Account > Subscriptions > select your plan > Cancel Subscription. Navigate through any retention screens — Chegg may show pause options, surveys, and discount offers before completing cancellation.
  3. Get a cancellation confirmation email. Chegg has a documented history of continuing charges after cancellation — monitor your next statement.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ FTC enforcement action ($7.5M settlement)

Chegg paid $7.5M to settle FTC charges in September 2025 for unlawful cancellation practices. The FTC found hidden links, pre-selected 'pause' options, mandatory surveys, and repeated attempts to dissuade cancellation — and continued charging customers who believed they had canceled.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:
