How to Cancel BarkBox

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online or email · Last verified: 2025-05-04 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to barkbox.com and sign in.
  2. Go to your account settings, find your subscription, and click 'Edit' then 'Cancel Renewal'.
  3. Alternatively, email BarkBox's 'Happy Team' at help@barkbox.com to request cancellation.
  4. ⚠️ Cancel by the first day of the month following your current subscription period to avoid the next charge. Check your billing cycle date before canceling.
