How to Cancel Hulu

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-01-23 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to hulu.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile name in the top right, then select 'Account'.
  3. Under 'Your Subscription', click 'Cancel'.
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm cancellation. If you subscribed through a third party (Spotify, Disney Bundle, etc.), you must cancel through that platform instead.
