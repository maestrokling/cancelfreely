How to Cancel eHarmony

Difficult (CFS 4/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-01-12 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to eharmony.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon > Account Settings > Billing.
  3. Find 'Cancel Subscription' or 'Turn Off Automatic Renewal'. eHarmony frames cancellation as 'turning off auto-renewal' rather than canceling. Your subscription continues until the current term ends.
  4. You will be required to enter your password and navigate multiple confirmation screens before cancellation is processed. Multiple screens with retention messaging before the final confirmation.
  5. If subscribed via Apple or Google, cancel through those platforms.

Dark Patterns

  • auto-renewal framing: eHarmony presents cancellation as 'turning off automatic renewal' rather than 'canceling your subscription' — obscuring the action and creating uncertainty about whether the account has been truly canceled.
  • multi-screen retention maze: eHarmony's cancellation flow includes password re-entry and multiple confirmation screens with retention messaging before reaching final cancel.
  • high price auto-renewal: eHarmony charges $35.90-$65.90/month and auto-renews at full price after introductory offers expire. Many users report surprise charges.

  • Offers a free trial extension.
