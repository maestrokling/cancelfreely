How to Cancel Sam's Club

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: in-store or phone · Last verified: 2026-01-12 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Visit any Sam's Club Member Service Desk in person. Immediate cancellation and prorated refund.
  2. Alternatively, call Sam's Club: 1-888-746-7726.
  3. Or request a refund online at samsclub.com.

Dark Patterns

  • credit card auto-renewal lock-in: Members who signed up with a Sam's Club credit card may find auto-renewal is tied to the card and cannot be canceled at the membership counter — they must cancel the credit card to stop auto-renewal.

Retention Tactics

  • None significant.
Notes: Sam's Club is a Walmart-owned warehouse club. Cancellation is straightforward in most cases. The credit card auto-renewal quirk is the main gotcha — if you have a Sam's Club credit card, you may need to address that separately.
