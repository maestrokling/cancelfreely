Cancellation Steps

  1. TurboTax is primarily pay-per-filing, not a recurring subscription. Most users pay once per tax year when they file. If you have TurboTax Advantage (auto-delivery of desktop software) or TurboTax Live, that is a recurring subscription.
  2. To cancel TurboTax Advantage: sign in at myturbotax.intuit.com > select 'My Account' > 'Manage TurboTax Advantage' > Cancel.
  3. To remove TurboTax Live/Expert Assist from your current return (before paying): go to your return > Review Your Order > Downgrade. If you've already used the Expert feature, you cannot remove it from that return.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ upsell during filing

TurboTax aggressively upsells to higher tiers and Expert Assist during the filing process. The downgrade path is intentionally less visible than the upgrade path.
