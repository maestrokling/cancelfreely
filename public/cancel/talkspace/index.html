How to Cancel Talkspace

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online or app · Last verified: 2026-01-14 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to talkspace.com or open the Talkspace app and sign in.
  2. Go to your account settings and select 'Cancel Plan' or 'Pause Plan'. Steps vary slightly depending on whether you have an out-of-pocket plan, insurance-covered plan, or employer/EAP benefits plan.
  3. For insurance or employer-covered plans: see help.talkspace.com for plan-specific instructions — the cancellation path differs.
  4. Talkspace allows plan pausing as an alternative to full cancellation.
