How to Cancel Verizon

Difficult (CFS 4/5)
Method: phone or in-store · Last verified: 2026-02-07 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Call Verizon: 1-800-922-0204. Say 'cancel service'. For Fios (internet/TV), call 1-800-837-4966.
  2. Alternatively, visit a Verizon store in person — generally faster than phone.
  3. Check your device payment plan status before calling. Any remaining device installment balance is due regardless of service cancellation. Number porting to a new carrier automatically cancels service. Port your number first if switching — this is the fastest cancellation path.
  4. Return Fios equipment (routers, set-top boxes) to avoid equipment fees.

Dark Patterns

  • device payment obligation confusion: Like AT&T, Verizon separates device installments from service billing. Customers who cancel before paying off a device still owe the full remaining balance, often hundreds of dollars.
  • retention specialist routing: Phone cancellations are routed through retention teams who make multiple offers before processing.

Retention Tactics

  • Offers loyalty discounts and bill credits.
  • Matches competitor pricing.
  • Offers an account pause instead of canceling.

Free Alternatives

  • Visible (Verizon network) — Budget wireless on Verizon's network, often $25/month
  • T-Mobile Home Internet — 5G home internet alternative to Fios
