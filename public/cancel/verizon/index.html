Cancellation Steps

  1. Call Verizon: 1-800-922-0204. Say 'cancel service'. For Fios (internet/TV), call 1-800-837-4966.
  2. Alternatively, visit a Verizon store in person — generally faster than phone.
  3. Check your device payment plan status before calling. Any remaining device installment balance is due regardless of service cancellation. Number porting to a new carrier automatically cancels service. Port your number first if switching — this is the fastest cancellation path.
  4. Return Fios equipment (routers, set-top boxes) to avoid equipment fees.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ device payment obligation confusion

Like AT&T, Verizon separates device installments from service billing. Customers who cancel before paying off a device still owe the full remaining balance, often hundreds of dollars.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers loyalty discounts and bill credits.
  • Matches competitor pricing.
  • Offers an account pause instead of canceling.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
