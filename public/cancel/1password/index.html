How to Cancel 1Password

Easy (CFS 1/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-01-21 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to 1password.com and sign in.
  2. Click your name > 'Manage Account' > 'Billing'.
  3. Click 'Cancel Subscription' and confirm. Your vault data is retained in read-only mode for 365 days after cancellation. You can still export your data.
