How to Cancel Hilton Grand Vacations

Very Hard — CFS 5/5
Method: certified mail or official exit program · Last verified: 2026-02-13 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. CRITICAL: Check your purchase contract for the rescission period — typically 3 to 10 days depending on your state. Act immediately if within this window.
  2. Within the rescission period: Write a formal rescission letter stating: your full name, contract number, purchase date, and the exact sentence 'I am writing to inform you of my decision to rescind my timeshare contract with Hilton Grand Vacations.' All signers on the contract must sign the letter.
  3. Send via USPS Certified Mail with Return Receipt to the cancellation address in your contract. Keep all tracking documentation permanently. Postmark must be before the rescission deadline — not delivery date.
  4. After the rescission period: Contact Hilton Grand Vacations owner services to ask about their official exit/deed-back program: 1-800-230-7068. Hilton has an official exit program for qualifying owners. Eligibility typically requires the account to be current on all payments.
  5. ⚠️ Do not pay any upfront fees to a third-party exit company. The FTC and state AGs have taken action against many of these.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
