How to Cancel Slack

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-03-15 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Sign into your Slack workspace on desktop. You must be a Workspace Owner or the person who originally upgraded the workspace.
  2. Click your workspace name in the top left, then select 'Tools & settings' > 'Workspace settings'.
  3. Go to the 'Billing' section.
  4. Select 'Cancel plan' or downgrade to the free tier. On annual plans, unused months may be refunded pro-rata. On monthly plans, access continues through the end of the billing period.
