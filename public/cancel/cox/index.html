How to Cancel Cox Communications

Difficult (CFS 4/5)
Method: phone or in-store · Last verified: 2025-11-29 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Call Cox: 1-800-234-3993. Cox does not offer online self-service cancellation. Phone or in-person required.
  2. Alternatively, visit a Cox store in person.
  3. State clearly you want to cancel. Retention agents will make discount offers. Cox has a 30-day satisfaction guarantee — cancel within 30 days for a full refund with no fees.
  4. Return all rented equipment (modem, router, cable boxes) to a Cox store or UPS. Get a receipt. Unreturned equipment results in charges of $150-300+ per device.

Dark Patterns
