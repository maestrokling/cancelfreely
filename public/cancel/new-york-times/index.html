How to Cancel New York Times

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: online or phone · Last verified: 2026-01-16 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to nytimes.com and sign in.
  2. Click your account name, then go to 'Account' > 'Manage Account'.
  3. Under your subscription, look for 'Cancel Subscription'. The cancel option may not be visible for all subscription types. Some users are routed to a chat or phone flow instead.
  4. If online cancellation is not available, call 1-800-698-4637 or use the live chat at help.nytimes.com. Phone/chat agents will make retention offers before completing cancellation.
  5. If subscribed via Apple, Amazon, or Google Play, cancel through those platforms instead.

Dark Patterns

  • online cancellation suppression: NYT does not offer online cancellation for all subscription types. Many users are routed to phone or chat, where they face retention pressure. This has been widely documented as a deliberate dark pattern.
  • introductory pricing trap: NYT heavily promotes introductory rates ($1-4/week for first year) that auto-renew at full price ($17-25+/week). Many users cancel after the intro period expires.

Retention Tactics

  • Phone/chat agents offer significant discounts (often 50-75% off) to stay.
  • Offers a pause on delivery or digital access.
  • Reminds you of Games, Cooking, Wirecutter access bundled in.

Free Alternatives

  • AP News — Free wire service news
  • Reuters — Free news wire
  • Public libraries — Many libraries offer free NYT digital access with a library card
