How to Cancel Carbonite

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-04-01 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to carbonite.com and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to 'Account Summary' and find the auto-renewal setting. Turn off automatic renewal. Your subscription remains active through the paid period, then expires.
  3. ⚠️ CRITICAL: After your subscription expires, Carbonite permanently deletes your backed-up files after a grace period of 30–60 days. Download any important backed-up files before canceling.
