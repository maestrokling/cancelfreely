Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to bookofthemonth.com and sign in.
  2. Go to Account > Membership settings.
  3. Select 'Cancel membership' and follow the prompts. Consider using the free 'Skip' option instead if you just need a break.
  4. Confirm cancellation. Any unused book credits are forfeited upon cancellation. Redeem them before canceling.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ skip-before-cancel maze

The cancellation flow strongly pushes the 'Skip a Month' option multiple times before allowing actual cancellation, making it unclear how to fully cancel.

⚠ credit forfeiture

Unused book credits (from add-on purchases or accumulated months) are forfeited upon cancellation. This creates sunk-cost pressure to stay subscribed.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Prominently highlights the free skip option as an alternative to cancellation.
  • Shows unused book credits that will be lost.
  • May offer a discounted rate or bonus credit to stay.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
