Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to amazon.com and sign in. Note: Canceling Prime Video alone is not straightforward — Amazon bundles video with the full Prime membership. To cancel video-only, you need to cancel Prime entirely or navigate to a specific page.
  2. Go to amazon.com/mc/pipelines/prime to access your Prime membership settings.
  3. Click 'Manage membership', then 'End membership'.
  4. Navigate through multiple confirmation screens — Amazon presents several offers and warnings before completing cancellation. You may see 3-4 screens with alternative offers (pause, discounts, reminders of benefits) before the final confirmation button.
  5. Click 'Cancel membership' to confirm.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ confirm-shaming button design

Cancellation screens rotate between having the 'yes cancel' and 'no keep my subscription' buttons swap their visual prominence (green vs gray), making it hard to know which button cancels vs keeps.

⚠ multi-screen maze

Amazon's cancel flow is notoriously long and arduous, with multiple screens presenting benefits, discounts, and pause options before confirming. The FTC sued Amazon over this practice in 2023.

⚠ bundling obscurity

Amazon Prime Video is bundled with Prime shipping, making it unclear how to cancel video-only access vs. the full Prime membership.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers to pause membership for 1-3 months.
  • Shows all Prime benefits (shipping, music, photos, etc.) you'll lose.
  • Offers a discounted membership rate.
  • Reminds you of upcoming delivery savings.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
