How to Cancel Nintendo Switch Online

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online or console · Last verified: 2026-01-21 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. On Nintendo Switch: System Settings > Nintendo eShop > your account > Nintendo Switch Online > Turn Off Auto-Renewal. Must be done at least 48 hours before renewal date.
  2. Online: Go to nintendo.com > sign in > your account > Shop Menu > Nintendo Switch Online > Turn Off Auto-Renewal.
  3. Membership remains active through the end of the paid period, then lapses. Online play, cloud saves, and NES/SNES library access are lost when membership lapses.
