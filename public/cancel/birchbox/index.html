How to Cancel Birchbox

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-03-06 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to birchbox.com and sign in.
  2. Go to Account Settings > Subscriptions.
  3. Select 'Cancel Subscription' and confirm. Cancellation stops auto-renewal. You'll still receive any box already billed for the current cycle.
  4. ⚠️ Renewals process on the 1st–5th of the month. Cancel before the 1st to avoid the next charge.
