How to Cancel HelloFresh

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-03-15 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to hellofresh.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon > 'Account Settings' > scroll down to 'Plan Settings'.
  3. Click 'Cancel Plan' and work through the cancellation flow. HelloFresh presents reason-based retention screens — the content varies based on your stated reason for leaving. Stay firm and keep clicking through.
  4. Confirm cancellation. You'll receive an email confirming cancellation. ⚠️ Cancel by 11:59 PM (local time) 5 days before your next delivery to avoid being charged for it.

Dark Patterns

Each reason triggers a tailored counter-argument before you reach the final cancel button.
  • cancellation deadline obscured: HelloFresh requires cancellation 5 days before the next delivery to avoid charges. This deadline is not prominently surfaced during cancellation.
  • historical dark patterns (settled): HelloFresh paid a $7.5M California settlement in 2025 for inadequate renewal disclosures and cancellation friction. The current flow has improved but still includes retention screens.

Retention Tactics

  • Offers a significant discount on the next box.
  • Shows tailored messaging based on your cancellation reason.
