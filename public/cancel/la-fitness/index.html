How to Cancel LA Fitness

Difficult (CFS 4/5)
Method: in-person or mail · Last verified: 2026-01-23 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Visit any LA Fitness location and request a cancellation form at the front desk. Unlike Planet Fitness, LA Fitness allows cancellation at any location — not just your home club.
  2. Complete and submit the cancellation form. Request a copy or confirmation for your records.
  3. Alternatively: download the LA Fitness cancellation form, complete it, and mail to: LA Fitness, Member Services, PO Box 54170, Irvine, CA 92619-4170. Use certified mail with return receipt. Include your full name, address, phone, date of birth, and membership ID.
  4. Some locations allow cancellation by fax: (949) 255-7200. Include all membership details.
  5. Confirm cancellation by checking your bank statement. LA Fitness typically requires 5 business days to process. Early termination fees may apply if under a contract term. Month-to-month members can cancel with no fee.

Dark Patterns

  • paper-only requirement: Online cancellation is not available for most members. The process requires in-person, mail, or fax — all of which create physical barriers.
  • contract early termination fees: Members on annual contracts face early termination fees, often not clearly disclosed at sign-up.

Retention Tactics

  • Front desk may offer a membership freeze or rate reduction instead of canceling.
