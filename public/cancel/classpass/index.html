Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to classpass.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon, then select 'Account Settings'.
  3. Select 'Plan' then 'Cancel plan'.
  4. Navigate through ClassPass's multi-step retention flow — reported as 8-10 steps by users. You'll be presented with pause options, credit rollover offers, and alternative plan downgrades before reaching the final cancel button.
  5. Select a cancellation reason and confirm. Any unused credits expire at the end of your billing period. Credits do not roll over after cancellation.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ excessive steps

Users report 8-10 required steps to complete cancellation, including multiple retention screens. One user documented being 'auto-enrolled into ClassPass's $89/month membership' and needing 8-10 steps to cancel.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers to pause membership for 1-3 months.
  • Offers a reduced credit plan instead of canceling.
  • Reminds you of unused credits that will expire.
  • Shows nearby studios and upcoming classes.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
