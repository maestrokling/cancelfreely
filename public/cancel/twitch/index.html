How to Cancel Twitch

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online or app · Last verified: 2026-03-01 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to twitch.tv and sign in. Twitch subscriptions are per-channel — you cancel each channel subscription separately.
  2. Go to twitch.tv/subscriptions to see all active channel subscriptions.
  3. Click the gear icon next to the subscription you want to cancel, then select 'Don't Renew Subscription'. The subscription remains active until the end of the paid period.
  4. If subscribed via mobile app store, cancel through Apple or Google instead.
