How to Cancel Medium

Easy (CFS 1/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2025-12-19 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to medium.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile picture, then select 'Settings'.
  3. Click 'Manage Subscription'.
  4. Click 'Cancel membership' and confirm. If you subscribed via the iOS app, you must cancel through Apple's subscription management. If via Google Play, cancel through Google Play.

Retention Tactics

  • May ask for feedback on why you're canceling before confirming.

Free Alternatives

  • Medium (free tier) — Limited free articles per month without a subscription
  • Substack — Free newsletter/blog reading; individual writers may charge
  • Dev.to — Free tech/dev focused writing platform
