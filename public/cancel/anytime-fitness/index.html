How to Cancel Anytime Fitness

Difficult — CFS 4/5
Method: in-person or mail · Last verified: 2026-01-27 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Contact your home club directly — Anytime Fitness is a franchise, so each location has its own cancellation process. Call or visit your specific club to get their exact cancellation requirements before proceeding.
  2. Visit your home club in person and request cancellation in writing.
  3. Alternatively: send a written cancellation notice via certified mail to your home club's address. Include your full name, membership ID, address, and a clear cancellation request. Use certified mail with return receipt.
  4. Provide required notice period — typically 30 days. You may be billed for one final month after notice. Early termination fee of up to $75 may apply if canceling before contract end.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ franchise variability

Each Anytime Fitness franchise sets its own cancellation policies, fees, and notice requirements. Corporate provides guidelines but individual clubs have discretion. This inconsistency makes it difficult to know what to expect.
