How to Cancel State Farm

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: phone or agent · Last verified: 2025-10-06 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. IMPORTANT: Have your new policy active before canceling. Never have a coverage gap.
  2. Contact your local State Farm agent directly — find them at statefarm.com or call 1-800-STATEFARM (1-800-782-8332). State Farm uses an agent-based model. Your agent is the primary contact for policy changes including cancellation.
  3. Provide your policy number and requested cancellation date. State Farm may ask for a written cancellation request in some cases.
  4. Unused premium is refunded pro-rata.

Retention Tactics

  • Your agent will note this as a retention opportunity — expect a rate review offer.
  • State Farm's website explicitly says agents 'will work with you to ensure you have the right coverage.'
Notes: State Farm is agent-based, which makes cancellation less friction-free than online-only insurers but generally straightforward. The agent relationship can work in your favor — they're often authorized to offer better rates to retain customers. Prorated refund is standard.
