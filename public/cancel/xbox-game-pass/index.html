How to Cancel Xbox Game Pass

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-02-15 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to account.microsoft.com and sign in.
  2. Go to 'Services & subscriptions' > find Xbox Game Pass > 'Manage' > 'Cancel subscription'.
  3. On Xbox console: Settings > Account > Subscriptions > select Xbox Game Pass > Manage > Cancel.
  4. Access continues through end of billing period. Downloaded games remain installed but unplayable after cancellation.
