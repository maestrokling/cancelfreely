Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: app store · Last verified: 2026-02-04 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. ⚠️ Deleting your Hinge account or the app does NOT cancel your subscription. You must cancel through the platform where you subscribed.
  2. iOS: Open Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions > Hinge > Cancel Subscription.
  3. Android: Open Google Play > Profile > Payments & Subscriptions > Subscriptions > Hinge > Cancel.
  4. Access continues through end of billing period.
