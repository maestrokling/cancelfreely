How to Cancel Norton 360

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-03-06 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to my.norton.com and sign in.
  2. Click your name or profile, then go to 'Subscription' or 'My Subscriptions'.
  3. Find your plan and click 'Turn off Auto-Renewal' or 'Cancel Subscription'. Norton frames cancellation as 'turning off auto-renewal' — your access continues through the paid period, then stops.
  4. If within 60 days of purchase: request a refund via chat at support.norton.com. Norton offers a 60-day money-back guarantee on annual plans.

Dark Patterns

  • auto-renewal framing: Norton presents cancellation as 'turning off auto-renewal' rather than canceling, obscuring whether the subscription has been ended. Community posts show widespread user confusion about this.
  • stealth auto-renewal charges: Norton charges auto-renewal fees, sometimes weeks before the renewal date. Users report charges they didn't expect, especially on accounts they believed were canceled.

Retention Tactics

  • May offer a discounted renewal rate.
