Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to ipsy.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile > 'Account Settings' > 'Membership'.
  3. Click 'Cancel Membership' and follow the prompts. ⚠️ Annual subscribers: You must cancel by 11:59 PM Pacific on the 25th of the last month of your subscription term to avoid auto-renewal. Example: if you subscribed in May 2025, cancel by April 25, 2026.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ annual renewal deadline trap

Annual Ipsy subscribers must cancel by a specific date (25th of the final month) or be auto-renewed for another full year. This deadline is buried in terms of service and not prominently surfaced during the subscription flow.
