Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to account.mcafee.com and sign in.
  2. Go to 'My Account' > 'Subscriptions' and look for auto-renewal settings. If the account page doesn't show a clear cancel option, contact support directly.
  3. Call McAfee support: 1-866-622-3911, or use live chat at mcafee.com/support. Phone/chat is often more reliable than the account portal for processing cancellation.
  4. If charged unexpectedly, request a full refund — agents are authorized to issue refunds for surprise renewals. Users report success getting full refunds by calling and citing an unexpected charge.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ zombie subscription auto-renewal

McAfee charges auto-renewal fees on accounts users believe are canceled or unused. Multiple Reddit reports of charges on accounts not used in years, often bundled with new PC purchases. Users report being charged on McAfee subscriptions pre-installed on new Windows PCs that they never intentionally activated.

⚠ shady renewal UI

McAfee's 'Renew subscription' link leads to upgrade options rather than renewal, and the auto-renewal opt-out is buried. Reddit documented 33 upvotes on a post titled 'McAfee shady renewal practices'.
