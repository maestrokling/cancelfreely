How to Cancel DoorDash DashPass

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online or app · Last verified: 2026-02-20 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Open the DoorDash app or go to doordash.com and sign in.
  2. Tap your profile icon > DashPass > Manage DashPass.
  3. Select 'Cancel DashPass' and confirm. If subscribed via Apple App Store or Google Play, cancel through those platforms instead.
  4. Access continues through end of billing period.

Dark Patterns

  • mobile billing redirect: Users who subscribed via iOS or Android must cancel through Apple/Google, not the DoorDash app or website.

Retention Tactics

  • May offer a discounted rate or free month.

Free Alternatives

  • DoorDash (no subscription) — Order without DashPass — delivery fees apply per order
Notes: DashPass is a low-friction cancel. Watch for the mobile billing redirect if you subscribed through an app store. DashPass is also bundled with some Chase credit cards — if you have a Chase Sapphire or similar, you may have DashPass included.
