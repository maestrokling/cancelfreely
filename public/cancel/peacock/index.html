How to Cancel Peacock

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-02-26 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to peacocktv.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon, then select 'Account'.
  3. Under 'Plan & Payment', click 'Change Plan'.
  4. Select 'Cancel Plan' and confirm. Some users report needing to contact customer support if online cancellation fails. Access continues until end of billing period.
