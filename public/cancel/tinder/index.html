Cancellation Steps

  1. ⚠️ CRITICAL: Deleting the Tinder app does NOT cancel your subscription. You will keep being charged.
  2. iOS: Open Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions > Tinder > Cancel Subscription.
  3. Android: Open Google Play > Profile > Payments & Subscriptions > Subscriptions > Tinder > Cancel.
  4. Web (if subscribed via tinder.com): Go to tinder.com > Profile > Settings > Manage Payment Account > Cancel Subscription.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ app deletion doesn't cancel

Deleting the Tinder app does not cancel the subscription — charges continue. This trips up a large number of users who assume deletion = cancellation.

⚠ cancellation method depends on signup platform

You must cancel through the same platform where you subscribed (Apple, Google, or web). If you subscribed via iOS and try to cancel in the app, there is no cancel button — you must go to Apple Settings.
