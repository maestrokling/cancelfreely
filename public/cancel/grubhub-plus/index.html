Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to grubhub.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon > 'Grubhub+' > 'Manage Membership'.
  3. Click 'Cancel Membership' (the link is light grey — look carefully).
  4. Click 'Continue cancellation' > 'Cancel membership' to confirm. Access continues through end of billing period.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ de-emphasized cancel link

The 'Cancel Membership' link is styled in light grey, making it visually hard to find compared to other options on the page.

⚠ missing cancel button

Some users report not being able to find the cancel button at all depending on account state. If the button is missing, contact Grubhub support via chat.
