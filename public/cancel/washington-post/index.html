How to Cancel Washington Post

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: online or chat · Last verified: 2026-04-02 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to washingtonpost.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile icon, then go to 'Account' > 'Manage subscription'.
  3. Look for 'Cancel subscription' — if not visible, use the live chat at help.washingtonpost.com. Some subscription types require chat to cancel.
  4. Chat agents will make retention offers. State clearly you want to cancel and do not want a discounted rate.

Dark Patterns

  • chat-required cancellation: Online self-service cancellation is not available for all subscribers. Many are routed to live chat, where retention agents make offers before processing cancellation.
  • introductory pricing trap: WaPo heavily promotes introductory rates ($1-4/month for 4-12 weeks) that auto-renew at $12-17/month. Subscriber churn has been significant following Bezos editorial changes.

Retention Tactics

  • Chat agents offer discounted rates (often $1-4/month) to stay.
  • Offers a temporary pause instead of canceling.

Free Alternatives

  • AP News — Free wire service
  • Public libraries — Many libraries offer free WaPo digital access
Notes: WaPo experienced multiple waves of mass subscriber cancellations in 2025 following editorial changes by owner Jeff Bezos. Cancellation friction is typical for major newspaper subscriptions. Library access is widely available.
