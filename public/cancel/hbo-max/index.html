How to Cancel HBO Max

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-02-15 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to max.com and sign in. The service rebranded from HBO Max to 'Max' in 2023. Cancellation steps are the same.
  2. Click your profile icon, then select 'Settings'.
  3. Select 'Subscription' or 'Billing'.
  4. Click 'Cancel Plan' and follow the prompts. If you subscribed through a TV provider, cable company, or third-party app store, you must cancel through that provider.

Dark Patterns

  • billing platform maze: Max has one of the most complex billing ecosystems of any streaming service — subscribers may come from Amazon, Apple, Roku, YouTube TV, AT&T, Hulu add-on, or direct billing. Cancellation path varies significantly by source, and the website doesn't always clearly identify which path applies to you.
  • price hike + complexity: Late 2025 price increases to $10.99/$18.49/$22.99 across tiers, combined with complex cancellation paths, led to significant user confusion.

Retention Tactics

  • Offers to pause subscription before canceling.
  • May present discounted plan offers.
  • Binge strategy: some guides recommend subscribing for one month, binging, then canceling — this is how many users approach Max.
