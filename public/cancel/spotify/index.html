How to Cancel Spotify

Moderate (CFS 3/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-01-26 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to spotify.com/account and sign in.
  2. Click 'Manage your plan' or go directly to spotify.com/account/subscription.
  3. Click 'Change or cancel' then select 'Cancel Premium'.
  4. Navigate through the retention screens — Spotify presents emotional appeals and FOMO content before the final confirmation.
  5. Click 'Yes, cancel' to confirm. You revert to the free tier (with ads) rather than losing access entirely.

Dark Patterns

  • emotional manipulation: Despite only requiring 5 clicks, Spotify uses guilt-trip messaging, 'what you'll miss' screens, and FOMO-based content previews during the cancellation flow. Under the FTC's Click-to-Cancel Rule (effective July 2025), some of these tactics may be non-compliant.
  • confusing button design: Some screens use emotionally loaded button labels (e.g., 'Keep my Premium' prominently styled vs. the cancel option).

Retention Tactics

  • Offers a discounted rate (often 1-3 months at reduced price) before confirming cancellation.
  • Shows personalized stats about your listening history to create emotional attachment.
  • Presents 'what you'll lose' including playlists, offline songs, and podcast downloads.

Free Alternatives

  • Spotify (free tier) — Full catalog access with ads and shuffle-only on mobile
  • YouTube Music (free tier) — Free with ads
  • Amazon Music (free tier) — Limited free tier included with Prime
Notes: Spotify doesn't remove access when you cancel Premium — you drop to the free ad-supported tier. This reduces the urgency of the cancellation decision. The emotional manipulation during the flow is well-documented but the actual process is not procedurally difficult.
