How to Cancel YNAB (You Need A Budget)

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online or app store · Last verified: 2026-03-03 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to app.youneedabudget.com and sign in.
  2. Click your account name > 'My Account' > 'Cancel your subscription'. If subscribed via iTunes, cancel through Apple Settings > Subscriptions instead.
  3. Confirm cancellation. Access continues through end of billing period. Your budget data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, then deleted.
