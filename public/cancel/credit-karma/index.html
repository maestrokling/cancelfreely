Cancellation Steps

  1. Credit Karma is free — there is no paid subscription to cancel. To delete your account: go to creditkarma.com and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings > Account > 'Close Account'. The close account option is somewhat buried — Reddit users note it requires some searching.
  3. Follow the prompts to confirm account deletion. Credit Karma will delete your account and personal data. Note: if you have a Credit Karma Money account (savings or spending), that must be closed separately first.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ buried account deletion

The account deletion option is not prominently surfaced. Reddit users describe it as 'a bit tricky' to find. Credit Karma (owned by Intuit) monetizes through financial product referrals — account closure is not incentivized.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • None — no paid subscription.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.

Notes: Credit Karma has no paid subscription — it's free and makes money through financial product referrals (credit cards, loans). If you want to stop using it, delete the account to remove your data. Close any Credit Karma Money accounts first before account deletion.
