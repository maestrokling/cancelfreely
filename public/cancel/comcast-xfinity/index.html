Cancellation Steps

  1. Option A (fastest): Take all equipment to your nearest Xfinity store and cancel in person. No hold times, no loyalty specialist. Reddit r/Comcast consensus: in-store is the fastest and least painful path. Bring all rented equipment.
  2. Option B: Call 1-800-XFINITY (1-800-934-6489) and say 'cancel service' to navigate to retention. Be prepared to hold and to decline multiple offers. Phone calls often route through a 'Loyalty Specialist' trained to retain you.
  3. Option C (some states): Online cancellation at xfinity.com/cancel — availability varies by state and account type. Comcast has blocked online cancellation for most customers despite having a fully online signup flow. Reddit posts document the lack of an online cancel option as of September 2024.
  4. Return all rented equipment (modem, router, cable boxes) within 10 days to avoid equipment fees. Return at an Xfinity store or via UPS. Get a receipt.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers significant bill discounts to stay (often 50% off for 12 months).
  • Offers a service pause instead of canceling.
  • Offers to move service to a new address.
  • Offers a downgrade to a lower-tier plan.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
