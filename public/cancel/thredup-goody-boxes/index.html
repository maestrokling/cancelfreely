Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to thredup.com and sign in to your account.
  2. Navigate to your account settings and find the Goody Box section, or go directly to thredup.com/goodybox.
  3. Click 'Cancel Subscription' or 'Skip/Cancel' under your active Goody Box subscription. Cancel before your next box ships to avoid the styling fee.
  4. If you have an active box in transit, return any items you do not want to keep within the return window (typically 14 days). You are only charged for items you keep. The styling fee is non-refundable.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ sunk cost styling fee

A non-refundable styling fee ($10) is charged per box regardless of whether you keep any items, which discourages cancellation by making users feel they should 'get their money's worth' from each box.

⚠ skip vs cancel confusion

The interface prominently offers 'Skip' as an alternative to canceling, and the skip option auto-resumes, which can lead to unexpected future charges for users who thought they had effectively stopped the service.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers a skip option (delay next box) as a prominent alternative to full cancellation.
  • Applies the styling fee as a credit toward purchases, incentivizing keeping items rather than canceling.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
