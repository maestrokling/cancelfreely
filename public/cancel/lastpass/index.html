How to Cancel LastPass

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-02-07 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to lastpass.com and sign in to your vault.
  2. Click your email/profile in the top right, then go to 'Account Settings' > 'Subscription'.
  3. Click 'Cancel Subscription' and follow the prompts. Account reverts to the free tier at end of billing period. Your vault data is preserved.

Free Alternatives

Notes: LastPass suffered a significant security breach in 2022 that exposed encrypted vault data. Many security professionals recommend migrating to Bitwarden or 1Password.
