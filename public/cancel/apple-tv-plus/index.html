How to Cancel Apple TV+

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-02-16 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to tv.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID. You can also cancel through iPhone/iPad: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Apple TV+ > Cancel Subscription.
  2. Click your account icon, then select 'Settings'.
  3. Scroll to 'Subscriptions' and click 'Manage'.
  4. Select Apple TV+ and click 'Cancel Subscription'. Confirm when prompted. Access continues through end of billing period.
