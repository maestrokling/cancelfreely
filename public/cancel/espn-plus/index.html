How to Cancel ESPN+

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online or app store · Last verified: 2026-03-01 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to espnplus.com or the ESPN app and sign in.
  2. Go to your profile > 'Manage' > 'Cancel Subscription'. If subscribed via Apple, Google, Roku, or Amazon, cancel through that platform.
  3. ⚠️ If you have the Disney Bundle (ESPN+ + Hulu + Disney+), you cannot cancel ESPN+ separately. You must cancel the entire bundle.
  4. Confirm cancellation. Access continues through end of billing period.
