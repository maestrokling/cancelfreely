Cancellation Steps

  1. Call SiriusXM: 1-866-635-2349. Be prepared for significant hold times. Online cancellation is technically available at siriusxm.com but widely reported as unreliable — many users end up in a loop or see errors. Phone is the only reliably working method.
  2. State clearly and repeatedly: 'I want to cancel my subscription completely.' SiriusXM retention agents are known for making 4–6 consecutive offers before processing cancellation. Decline all offers firmly.
  3. Get a cancellation confirmation number and note the agent's name. ⚠️ SiriusXM has a documented history of continuing to charge after cancellation. Check your statement the following month.
  4. If charged after cancellation: call back with your confirmation number and request a refund. If refused, dispute the charge with your bank.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Offers 3 free months.
  • Offers 50%+ discount for 6-12 months.
  • Offers to remove specific channels to lower price.
  • Transfers to a 'loyalty specialist' to make additional offers.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
