How to Cancel Headspace

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: app store or web · Last verified: 2026-01-24 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Cancel method depends on where you subscribed — check your original purchase confirmation email.
  2. iOS: Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions > Headspace > Cancel Subscription.
  3. Android: Google Play > Profile > Payments & Subscriptions > Subscriptions > Headspace > Cancel.
  4. Web (direct): headspace.com > Profile > Settings > Subscription > Cancel.
