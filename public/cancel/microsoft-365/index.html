How to Cancel Microsoft 365

Simple (CFS 2/5)
Method: online · Last verified: 2026-01-15 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Go to account.microsoft.com and sign in.
  2. Click 'Services & subscriptions'.
  3. Find Microsoft 365 and click 'Manage'.
  4. Select 'Cancel subscription' and follow the prompts to confirm. Access continues through the end of the current billing period. Files saved in OneDrive remain accessible in read-only mode for a limited period after cancellation.

Free Alternatives

  • LibreOffice — Free open-source Office suite
  • Google Workspace (free tier) — Free Docs, Sheets, Slides with Google account
  • OnlyOffice (free) — Free Office-compatible suite
Notes: Microsoft 365 personal/family cancellation is straightforward. Business subscriptions through the Microsoft 365 admin center follow a different process. After cancellation, OneDrive storage drops to the free 5GB tier — files above that limit become inaccessible after a grace period.
