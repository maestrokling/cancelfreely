How to Cancel Planet Fitness

Difficult — CFS 4/5
Method: in-person or certified mail · Last verified: 2026-02-05 · Official site

Cancellation Steps

  1. Visit your home club in person — the specific location where you originally signed up. You CANNOT cancel at a different Planet Fitness location. If you've moved, you must use the certified mail method instead.
  2. Request a cancellation form at the front desk and fill it out completely. Some locations require a manager to process the cancellation. If no manager is available, you may be asked to return.
  3. Alternatively: send a written cancellation letter via certified mail with return receipt requested to your home club's address. Include your full name, address, phone number, membership ID, and a clear request to cancel. Use certified mail — regular mail may be claimed as 'never received.'
  4. Confirm cancellation by checking your bank statement in the next billing cycle. Planet Fitness bills on the 17th of each month. Canceling after the 17th may result in one additional charge. There is also an annual fee billed around August — cancel before the 25th of the prior month to avoid it. Early termination fee of ~$58 may apply if under contract.

⚠ Watch Out — Dark Patterns

⚠ in-person requirement

Online cancellation is not available despite the FTC's Click-to-Cancel Rule (in legal limbo as of 2026 due to gym industry lobbying). Members must visit their home club or send certified mail.

What to Expect (Retention Tactics)

If you contact support, they will likely offer:

  • Front desk staff often offer to freeze the membership (typically $0-5/month) instead of canceling.
  • May offer a reduced rate to stay.

You do not need to accept any of these. Say: "I would like to cancel my account completely." Repeat as needed. You do not need to provide a reason.
